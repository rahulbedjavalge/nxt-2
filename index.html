How Nxt Action Works

A guided workflow turns regulations into practical tasks and evidence tracking.

01

Setup scope

Select country, sector, and company size to preload relevant regulations.

02

Upload sources

Drag-and-drop documents or link cloud storage for OCR and parsing.

03

Extract obligations

AI identifies must-do actions, deadlines, and evidence requirements.

04

Track & prove

Assign tasks, collect proof, and export audit-ready reports.

Extraction Pipeline

  • Parser → OCR + structure extraction
  • Classifier → detects must/shall/required obligations
  • Synthesizer → drafts tasks with priority + reason
  • Citations → links back to source sections
